Loose Leaf Tea

Do you grow herbs? Tea leaves? Got a Hibiscus growing somewhere? We can make tea!

Ingredients

  • Leaves of choice (Black, Passionflower, Chamomile, Hibiscus, Orange Peels, etc.)
  • Honey
  • Sugar
  • Creamer

Steps:

  1. Dry leaves either with a dehydrator or a shelf free of dust.
  2. Strip leaves from the stem. Though some stems, like mint, have a higher concentration of flavor. However, young stems are best because older stems become more fibrous and woody.
  3. Using an infuser, tea strainer, or teapot brew the tea for about 5-10 minutes. Longer than teabags, typically. I use an infuser.
  4. Add your milk, creamer, sugar. I love oat milk! That's it. Thank you.
